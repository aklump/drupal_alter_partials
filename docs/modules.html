Provide Partials by Module

  1. Your modules can also provide alter partials but they must declare themselves using hook_alter_partials_info. See alter_partials.api.php for documentation.
  2. Implement hook_alter_partials_info in your module.
  3. Follow the theme instructions, except that you will create that files inside your module's alter_partials directory.
